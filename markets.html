Steam

Steam is a video game digital distribution service by Valve. It was launched as a standalone software client in September 2003 as a way for Valve to provide automatic updates for their games, and expanded to include games from third-party publishers. Steam offers digital rights management (DRM), server hosting, video streaming, and social networking services. It also provides the user with installation and automatic updating of games, and community features such as friends lists and groups, cloud storage, and in-game voice and chat functionality.

Facebook Marketplace

Facebook Marketplace is a place where people can discover, buy and sell items listed on Facebook within their local community. It is a convenient destination to discover, buy and sell items with people in your community. Facebook Marketplace is available in 50 countries, and is used by 800 million people every month.
