Plot

Cyberpunk 2077 is a first-person perspective RPG with a mature story set in the vast open-world of Night City. The game follows the story of V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. You can customize your character’s cyberware, skillset and playstyle, and explore a vast city where the choices you make shape the story and the world around you.

About Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure role-playing video game, developed and published by CD Projekt RED that was released December 10, 2020. The game was announced during the 2012 CD Projekt Red Summer Conference as the official video game adaption of Mike Pondsmith's pen and paper RPG Cyberpunk 2020. An expansion for the videogame, called Phantom Liberty, was released on September 26, 2023.

Phantom Liberty

Phantom Liberty is a new forthcoming addition to Cyberpunk 2077. It will feature a spy-thriller story set in a whole new area of Night City. As V, take on an impossible mission of espionage and survival. With new skills, new weapons and new tech, plunge deep into a web of broken loyalties and political power plays.

Cyberpunk Edgerunners

Cyberpunk 2077: Edgerunners is an anime series set in the dark, futuristic world of Night City, the same setting as the video game Cyberpunk 2077. The show follows David Martinez, a street kid turned mercenary outlaw known as an "edgerunner." As David navigates the dangerous underbelly of the city, he encounters colorful characters, advanced technology, and corporate intrigue.
The series explores themes of transhumanism, social inequality, and the cost of ambition in a world where cybernetic enhancements blur the line between human and machine. With its vibrant animation style, intense action sequences, and gritty storytelling, Edgerunners expands on the Cyberpunk universe while standing as its own compelling narrative.
